Peak Voltage: The peak voltage after which we say with certainty that an action potential has occurred (mV).


Threhold Voltage (mv): This determines when a neuron will start a divergent change in voltage that will tend toward infinity and is not the voltage at which we consider the neuron to have spiked. External factors can cause an action potential to fail even if v_mem > v-Th.


Reset Voltage (mV): Defaults to 3-spike bursting behavior at .8 nA current.
