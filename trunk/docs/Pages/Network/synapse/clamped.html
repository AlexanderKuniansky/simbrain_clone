Clamped Syanpse

Clamped synapses do not change their value. Learning is not possible with these synapses. Setting one or more synapses to clamped is useful when focusing on neural dynamics only.

Sometimes it is useful to temporarily clamp a group of synapses, without change the types of those synapses. See Network > Edit > Clamp weights.
