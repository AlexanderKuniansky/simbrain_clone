IAC Neuron

These nodes get their name from the IAC or "Inter-Active Competition" network which John McLelland and David Rummelhart in the 1980's, and later featured as a prominent example in the PDP volumes. IAC networks continue to be used today, in particular to study psychological proceses. Activation of these nodes is often interpreted as degree of belief in some hypothesis, while connection represent evidentiary relationships between these beliefs.
