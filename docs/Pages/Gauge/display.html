Display

Pan and Zoom

The HiSee display is currently 2-dimensional. It is based on the Piccolo zoomable user interface (ZUI), which allows users to pan and zoom and graphical data. When autoscale in the preference menu is turned off, you can pan the visible data by left-dragging (dragging the mouse while holding the left-mouse button down), and you can zoom in or out on data by right-dragging (dragging the mouse while holding the right button down).

Clicking on points

You can also click on data points to reveal which high-dimensional points they correspond to (results will appear in the terminal window).
