Lab: Dynamical Systems

Make a network with four nodes, fully interconnected. You can leave the nodes and weights at their default values (clamped, and linear).

Now look for attractors and limit cycles.

Say:

 

1) How many dimensions is the state space

2) How many dimensions is the weight space

3) How many attractors did you find?

4) How many repellors did you find?

5) How many limit cycles did you find?

6) For each limit cycle, say what its period is.
